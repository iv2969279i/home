5. Investment Revenue Share Calculation

  • No revenue share is charged unless a transaction is completed. Upon receipt of the first deposit from the client, the provider must pay the investment revenue share within 3 business days. A 2.5% late fee per day applies after that.
  • Investment revenue shares are not deducted from the provider’s quote but are added on top of the provider’s original price.
  • For the first transaction with a new client, the investment revenue share is 36.5% of the provider's quoted price.
  • For repeated transactions with the same client, the revenue share decreases by 2.5% each time, down to a minimum of 6.5%.
  • Example: If your service quote is 100,000 JPY, the client will pay 136,500 JPY.
